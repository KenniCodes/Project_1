What is Pokémon?

Pocket Monsters, better known as, Pokémon™ was developed by Game Freak© and published in Japan by Nintendo©. Pokémon Red Version and Pokémon Blue Version were released in 1996. Set in the Kanto region and featuring 151 Pokémon to collect, battle and trade. The main goal being to obtain all 151 Pokémon, defeat all eight gym leaders plus the elite four and finally the champion. Since then there have been many core series entries introducing new generations of Pokémon, which is currently at eight generations and one in development.

Breakdown of games that take place in the same region

Each videogame has Pokémon which are unique to each region. And each Pokémon region is ispired by a real world location. For example the Kanto region is based off of Japan, specifically the Izu Islands & Bonin Islands. However there is crossover and variation to Pokémon in some regions.
